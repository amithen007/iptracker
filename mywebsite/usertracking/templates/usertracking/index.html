What is Phishing?

Phishing is a method used by cybercriminals to deceive individuals into divulging sensitive information, typically through fake websites or deceptive emails.

How to Protect Yourself?

1. Always verify the sender of any suspicious emails before clicking on any links.
2. Avoid visiting unfamiliar websites or providing personal information on questionable sites.
3. Use multi-factor authentication (MFA) on your accounts wherever possible.
4. Regularly update your software to patch security vulnerabilities.
5. Be cautious of any pop-ups or unexpected messages asking for your credentials.
